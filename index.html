My Story

Hello, my name is Adam Jeske, I am 34 years old and I was born and raised in Sacramento, CA. I love to be outdoors more often than not, be it skateboarding, mountain biking, hiking whatever, just anything to get me outside. I also prefer the mountains over the city more times than not, even in the winter when they are covered in snow because that just means fresh powder for shredding.

For the last few years I have been working in the music and entertainment industry on the logistical side of touring. I work with the road crew to ensure that they have what they need for the tour. I had dabbled in coding in the past on my own using things like code school, etc. But after a few unhappy years in my current industry I am ready to take my skills to the next level and look for a career change.
